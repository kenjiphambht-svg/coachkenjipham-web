# Child Language Rule

Read `docs/brand/CHILD_LANGUAGE_RULES.md` before changing child-related pages, forms, reports, CTAs, or product copy.

Do not label a child, predict the child's future, spiritualize the child, assign destiny or fixed identity, or make parents feel guilty or afraid.

Use soft observational language such as khuynh hướng, gợi ý quan sát, cha mẹ có thể để ý, một cửa sổ để hiểu con, and hạt mầm bản sắc.
