# Frontend Quality Rule

Prioritize mobile quality, accessibility, performance, and maintainability.

Use existing dependencies first. Do not add animation libraries unless explicitly approved.

Motion should be restrained: mist, breath, warm light, slow reveal, and subtle hover states.

Premium Essence screens must follow three principles:

- Mỗi màn hình một ý.
- Khoảng trống là vật liệu chính.
- Chuyển động chỉ ở mức hơi thở.

Check mobile and desktop layouts, CTA clarity, readable spacing, navigation behavior, and that text does not overflow or overlap.
