# Child Language Rules

These rules apply to Bản Sắc Của Con, Bản Sắc Hạt Mầm, child-related publications, parent-facing product pages, forms, and follow-up copy.

## Do Not

- Do not label a child.
- Do not predict the child's future.
- Do not spiritualize the child.
- Do not assign destiny or fixed identity.
- Do not make parents feel guilty or afraid.

## Use Soft Observational Language

Prefer language that keeps the child open, changing, and human.

Useful phrases:

- khuynh hướng
- gợi ý quan sát
- cha mẹ có thể để ý
- một cửa sổ để hiểu con
- hạt mầm bản sắc

## Writing Notes

- Frame insights as possibilities to observe, not facts to impose.
- Keep parents supported, not judged.
- Avoid fear-based urgency.
- Avoid deterministic language.
- Protect the child's dignity and future openness.
