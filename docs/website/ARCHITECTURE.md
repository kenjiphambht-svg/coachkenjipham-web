# Website Architecture

coachkenjipham.com is the official public website and brand hub for Kenji Phạm and Essence Coaching System. The site should stay simple on the surface and clear in navigation, while allowing deeper content and systems to exist behind the correct doors.

## Public Main Routes

- `/` - Homepage / Brand Hub / routing hall
- `/ve-kenji` - Kenji identity and credibility page
- `/phuong-phap` - How Essence works, public-safe explanation
- `/bat-dau` - Light entry route, ebook/quiz later; do not open strongly until content exists
- `/goc-doc` - Ghi chép Essence, content/SEO/AIO/GEO engine
- `/ban-sac-cua-con` - Parent/child product hub
- `/an-pham-ban-sac-hat-mam` - Current public landing for the 0-7 product
- `/ai-startup` - AI Startup Dossier, already exists; do not redesign unless asked
- `/lien-he` - Contact
- `/dieu-essence-khong-hua` - Ethical boundaries page
- `/chinh-sach-rieng-tu` - Privacy policy, especially important because child/family data is involved

## Conditional Or Future Routes

- `/phien-90-lang` - Only open if Kenji is ready to accept adult coaching clients
- `/phan-tich-2-lop` - Only open if delivery/report process is ready
- `/hanh-trinh-90-ngay` - Should not have direct purchase; form/discussion only
- `/tu-dien-essence` - Future public glossary / GEO asset

## Private And Noindex Routes

- `/an-pham/[random-slug]` - Private reading room for personalized publications; noindex; not in nav; not in sitemap
- `/thanh-toan-*` - Payment pages; noindex
- `/fcp` - Deprecated; do not promote; redirect or noindex later

## Navigation

Main navigation should stay simple:

- Essence
- Bắt đầu
- Bản Sắc Của Con
- Ghi chép
- AI Startup
- Liên hệ

## Routing Principle

Do not expose more choices than a visitor can confidently understand. The homepage should route visitors into three flows:

- For themselves
- For their child
- For understanding Kenji / Essence / the AI system
